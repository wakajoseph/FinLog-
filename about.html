About FinLog

FinLog Expense Tracker

Take control of your finances with intelligent tracking and insightful analytics

FinLog is a cloud-based expense tracking and financial management app designed to help users monitor spending, plan budgets, and achieve financial goals. Since it is internet-dependent, all your data is synchronized securely across devices, providing seamless access anywhere.

Our Mission

To empower users to take control of their finances, understand spending patterns, and make smarter decisions with a simple, intuitive interface.

Our Vision

To become the most user-friendly, secure, and insightful expense tracker for individuals who want to track, save, and plan without hassle.

Key Features

Track Expenses

Record daily spending, categorize transactions, and monitor cash flow in real-time with detailed insights.

Budget Planning

Set budgets for categories like food, transport, and entertainment to manage spending efficiently.

Security & Privacy

We recommend using alternate account details. Passwords and PINs are securely stored with encryption.

Reports & Insights

Visualize your spending trends and get insights to make better financial decisions.

Privacy & Security

  • Do not use real banking accounts or sensitive personal data.
  • Create strong passwords and PINs. Do not share them with anyone.
  • All data is encrypted and stored on secure servers with industry-standard protection.
  • Your information is never sold to third parties.
  • Regular security audits ensure your data remains protected.

Usage Notes

  • FinLog requires an active internet connection for syncing data across devices.
  • Always log out on shared devices and secure your account credentials.
  • We are not responsible for losses caused by misuse of the app or insecure account information.
  • Regular updates ensure you have the latest features and security enhancements.
